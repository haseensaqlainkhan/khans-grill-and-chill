How the night flows

No Timeline.
Just BBQ Chaos,
But Classy.

No "7:15 starters, 8:30 mains" business. The grill has one rule: if it is hot, it gets eaten. Hover over the cards — they move with the fire.

🥖
Bread Sizzling
Butter bread and garlic butter bread hit the grill first, because waiting hungry is not a personality trait.
🍖
Meat On Rotation
Chicken, beef, and mutton come out in smoky batches. Someone will say "just one more piece" at least six times.
🥤
Drinks On Ice
Fresh lime soda, Thums Up, Sprite, and the zero-sugar gang standing by for serious refreshment duties.
🌿
Chutney Politics
Mint chutney people, mayo people, peri peri people — everyone gets a side. Nobody needs to be civil about it.
🌽
Sides Sneak In
Corn, potatoes, pineapple — making an appearance for balance. Mostly so we can pretend this was planned.
🔥
Grill Supervisors
There will be at least two self-appointed experts near the fire. Their qualifications: confidence and a plate.
🌙
Terrace Breeze
Bangalore evening air, smoke drifting up, lights glowing low, and everyone slowly forgetting what time it is.
✨
Second Rounds
The official schedule says nothing. The unofficial schedule says another round when the next batch smells too good.

"Attendance is free.
Missing it is not."
