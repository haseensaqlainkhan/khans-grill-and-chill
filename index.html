How the night flows

No Timeline.
Just BBQ Chaos,
But Classy.

No "7:15 starters, 8:30 mains" business. The grill has one rule: if it is hot, it gets eaten. Hover over the cards — they move with the fire.

🥖
Bread Sizzling
Butter bread and garlic butter bread hit the grill first. Every BBQ needs a proper opening act.
🍖
Meat On Rotation
Chicken, beef, and mutton come out in smoky batches. The grill keeps moving, and so do the plates.
🥤
Drinks On Ice
Fresh lime soda, Thums Up, Sprite, and the zero-sugar crew standing by to keep things cool.
🌿
Chutney Politics
Mint chutney, mayo, and green chilli coriander. Different tastes, one plate at a time.
🌽
Sides Join The Party
Corn, potatoes, pineapple — bringing colour, balance, and surprisingly strong fan followings.
🔥
Grill Supervisors
There will be at least two self-appointed experts near the fire. Their qualifications: confidence and a plate.
🌙
Terrace Breeze
Bangalore evening air, smoke drifting up, lights glowing low, and everyone slowly forgetting what time it is.
✨
Second Rounds
The official schedule says nothing. The unofficial schedule says another round when the next batch smells too good.
